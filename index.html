Research and Project Experiences

Netflix Data Analysis

The project involves analyzing Netflix user data using Excel for initial data cleaning, Python for advanced analysis and visualizations, Power BI for interactive dashboards, and MySQL for database management. The aim is to uncover viewing patterns, user behavior, and content trends to drive data-driven business insights.

Innovative Approach of Teaching Ray Optics in the Classroom

This project focuses on developing an innovative teaching methodology for Ray Optics, enhancing student engagement through interactive demonstrations and visual aids. The approach was tested in classrooms, showing a 65% improvement in students' understanding and retention of key concepts. The article can be accessed here.

Exploring Chaotic Behaviour of Impact of Climate Change on Insurance Models

It explores the chaotic behavior of climate change's impact on insurance models, using chaos theory and statistical methods to analyze unpredictability in risk assessment. The goal is to develop predictive models that account for climate-induced volatility, helping insurers better manage long-term risks.

Coordinate Geometry in 2D designing

This project applies principles of coordinate geometry to 2D design, leveraging geometric equations to create precise and visually appealing structures. The focus is on using algebraic techniques to model and render designs, offering a mathematical foundation for graphic and architectural applications.

Synchronization of Chaos in Electronic Circuits (Undergraduate Thesis)

Modeling chaotic systems using Wolfram Mathematica to analyze their dynamic behavior. The research contributes to understanding how chaotic circuits can be synchronized, with potential applications in secure communications and signal processing.

Analyzing the Success of Mega Optics Outreach Program using Novel Mathematical Methods

UsedPython and Mathematica for data analysis of improvements based on pre-test, post-test, and feedback data. Our findings inform policies for officials in the sector.

Analysis and Study of trends in gut microbiota

Analyzed gut bacteria extinction in urban populations compared to tribal communities, focusing on sustainable development and 16S ribosomal RNA Sequence Clusters.

Application of Subsets & Bayesian Theorem (Bayes Naive, specifically) in Spam Detection

Applied subsets & Naive Bayes, utilizing probability theory to classify emails based on word frequencies and patterns. The model improves the accuracy of filtering spam by identifying key features, making it a robust tool for automated email categorization.
